About Me

Shaylene Liao

Hi! I’m Shaylene. I am a San Francisco native who loves smart marketing, witty captions, and creative community building. Three words that describe me are: friendly, imaginative, and resilient.

Right now, I am a fourth-year at the University of California, Berkeley majoring in Media Studies/Mass Communications. My work experience includes being ZeroCater’s first and only intern. A startup based in downtown San Francisco focused on connecting businesses with local restaurants and consumer brands to provide top notch perks while also promoting healthy workplace habits. As the Public Relations and Social Media intern, I was also afforded the luxury of being the only intern in the office which allowed for undivided attention for my education. My duties included; managing and creating a social media content calendar, owning customer-engagement on social and review channels, conducting research to identify pitch angles, and assisting on media drop campaigns.

In the startup culture, I gained valuable firsthand experience working effectively within cross-functional teams while also obtaining an incredible sense of responsibility. I feel so fortunate to be from San Francisco, and I try to take advantage of everything that the Bay Area has to offer. When I am not hitting the books, you’re likely to find me at a local coffee shop or exploring nature (I’m a sucker for waterfall hikes).
